How to Join an Orbit

1

Join the Discord Server

Connect to a server that has OrbitKin installed. The bot will welcome you automatically.

2

Complete Your Signal

Use /questionnaire to map your unique Signal through our personality assessment.

3

Wait for Your Match

Our algorithm finds your perfect Orbit. You'll be notified when your Kin are ready to meet!

4

Connect with Your Kin

Join your private Orbit channel and start building meaningful friendships with Orbot's guidance.
