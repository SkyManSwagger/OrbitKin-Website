Glossary & Terms

Understanding OrbitKin terminology

OrbitKin (OK)

The name of the service, application, and the overarching community. 'OK' is used as a casual, memorable shorthand for the brand.

Orbot

The friendly, user-facing AI guide within OrbitKin. Orbot helps users navigate their journey, understand their Signal, and connect with Orbits.

Orbits

The curated small groups of 4-6 members formed by the service. These are safe, supportive spaces for connection.

Kin

The individuals you are matched with; your fellow group members within an Orbit. This term evokes a sense of close, chosen family and deep connection.

Signal

A user's unique profile, encompassing their preferences, values, interests, and assessment data. It's what OrbitKin 'reads' to find resonance.

Kindreds

The universal, inclusive term for all users of OrbitKin, fostering an immediate sense of community and shared purpose from the moment they join.

Star-Seekers

Users during their active journey of discovery within OrbitKin – specifically during onboarding, Signal Mapping, and while awaiting Orbit matching.

Pathfinders

Users who are actively forging new connections, overcoming challenges, or highlighting the innovative and guiding nature of OrbitKin itself.

Alignment

The process by which OrbitKin's sophisticated system successfully matches compatible user Signals, bringing them into an optimal configuration for connection.

Resonance

The profound feeling of deep compatibility, mutual understanding, and harmonious connection experienced between Kin within a well-aligned Orbit.

Charting/Mapping

The process of completing your profile and assessments. It's an act of self-exploration that enables connection with others.

Cosmic Tech-Magic

OrbitKin's unique theme blending cosmic wonder, elegant technology, and the 'magic' of human connection. Based on psychological principles and data, not astrology.

Attunement

A fine-tuning process that brings Signals and Kin into harmony, speaking to the sensitivity and precision of connection.

Convergence

When individual paths or Signals meet and come together to form something new and meaningful - an Orbit.

Synchrony

When elements like interests, communication styles, or availability come together harmoniously, creating smooth and compatible group dynamics.
